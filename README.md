#  Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Online Store Sales Performance

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on a detailed analysis of e-commerce operational data. The goal is to evaluate sales trends, identify high-performing product categories, and analyze geographical distribution to provide actionable business insights.

## Tech Stack
- **Language:** Python 3.x
- **Data Manipulation:** Pandas, NumPy
- **Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn
- **Environment:** Google Colab

## Key Analysis Phases

### 1. Sales Dynamics & Seasonality
Analysis of monthly revenue trends to identify peak performance periods.
* **Visualization:** Multi-line charts showcasing revenue growth across different product types.
* **Finding:** Identified seasonal spikes in specific categories like 'Office Supplies' and 'Cosmetics'.

### 2. Product Category Performance
A deep dive into which categories drive the most value.
* **Metric:** Total Revenue and Profit per category.
* **Finding:** While 'Office Supplies' leads in order volume, categories like 'Household' and 'Cosmetics' show higher overall revenue contribution.

### 3. Geographical Distribution
Mapping sales performance across different countries to identify core markets.
* **Focus:** Top 10 countries by order volume.
* **Insight:** Strong market presence established in European regions, specifically highlighting Czech Republic, Romania, and Greece.

### 4. Profitability & Revenue Correlation
Using Scatter Plots to visualize the relationship between total sales and net profit.
* **Objective:** Identifying high-margin products and potential outliers that drive business growth.
